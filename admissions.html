Admission

Your Child’s Journey Starts Here

We are delighted that you are considering Gopal Krishna School for your child. Our admissions team is here to guide you through every step of the process, ensuring a smooth and transparent experience.

01

Schedule a Campus Tour

We invite you to visit our campus to experience our vibrant community and state-of-the-art facilities firsthand. A guided tour is the best way to see our students and faculty in action.

02

Submit Application

After your tour, complete our online application form. We require this, along with a few documents, to understand your child's educational background and learning needs

03

Interview & Assessment

We will schedule a personal meeting with you and your child. This is an opportunity for us to get to know each other and for us to understand your child’s personality and learning style.

04

Enrollment Confirmation

Upon a successful interview, you will receive an acceptance letter. The final step is to pay the enrollment fee to confirm your child’s admission to Gopalkrishna School.

Required enrollment documents

To complete your child’s admission, please ensure you have the following documents ready. Our admissions team will guide you on how and when to submit them.

Begin Application
  • Completed Application Form
  • Child's Birth Certificate
  • Previous School Transfer Certificate (if applicable)
  • Passport-size Photographs of Student and Parents
  • Medical Fitness Certificate
  • Aadhar Card or other Proof of Address
