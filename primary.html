Curriculum & Subjects

English Language Arts
Our English curriculum focuses on developing strong reading comprehension, fluent writing, effective grammar, and confident oral communication skills through diverse literary texts and creative writing exercises.
Mathematics
Students delve into number systems, arithmetic operations, geometry, measurement, and data handling. The focus is on conceptual understanding and applying mathematical principles to real-world problems.
Science
Through hands-on experiments and observational learning, students explore fundamental concepts in Physics, Chemistry, and Biology, fostering scientific inquiry and a deeper understanding of the natural world.

Our Primary Teaching Methodology

Our teaching methodology for Primary students is designed to be engaging, interactive, and effective, fostering a deep understanding and love for learning.

Inquiry-Based Learning

We seamlessly integrate educational technology into the curriculum to enhance learning, provide interactive experiences, and prepare students for the digital world. .

Collaborative Projects

Students work together on projects, promoting teamwork, communication, and problem-solving skills, while learning from their peers.

Technology Integration

Recognizing that each child is unique, our educators provide personalized attention, adapting teaching strategies to cater to individual learning styles and paces, ensuring every child thrives.

Continuous Assessment

Our assessment methods are continuous and formative, providing regular feedback to students and parents, supporting individual progress and identifying areas for growth.
